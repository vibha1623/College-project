COLOURS

Colours is a college club dedicated to supporting student mental health. It creates a safe and inclusive space for open discussions, workshops, and educational sessions on topics like stress management, mindfulness, and self-care. The club organizes awareness campaigns to reduce stigma, promotes peer support networks, and encourages the formation of healthy relationships. It offers relaxation activities such as yoga, art therapy, and outdoor excursions to help students unwind. Colours collaborates with other organizations to expand its reach and provide comprehensive resources. Through its initiatives, Colours strives to foster a supportive community that prioritizes mental well-being and empowers students to seek help when needed.
