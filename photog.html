PHOTOG(The Photography Club)

Photog is a dynamic college club that immerses students in the captivating world of photography. With a keen eye for detail, the club captures various events, workshops, and activities happening in the college, preserving cherished memories. Photog aims to enhance photography skills by organizing regular workshops where members can learn techniques, explore different styles, and experiment with their creativity. The club also takes charge of organizing photography-centric events during the college's annual cultural fest, Confluence, showcasing the power of visual storytelling. Through its passion for photography, Photog encourages students to see the world through a lens, capturing moments that inspire and evoke emotions.
