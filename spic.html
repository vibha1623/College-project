SPICMACAY

SPICMACAY (Society for the Promotion of Indian Classical Music and Culture Amongst Youth) is a vibrant college club dedicated to promoting and preserving Indian classical music and culture among students. The club organizes events such as Sarang, where renowned classical musicians perform, mesmerizing the audience with their talent and expertise. SPICMACAY also curates exciting competitions like Bands of Battles, providing a platform for student bands to showcase their musical prowess. Additionally, the club encourages solo singing, nurturing individual talents and fostering a love for Indian classical and folk music. Through its initiatives, SPICMACAY aims to create a deeper appreciation for India's rich musical heritage and cultural diversity among the youth.
