MAD(Managing And Directing club)

MAD (Managing And Directing club) is a vibrant college club dedicated to the seamless organization of various events and cultural festivities. With a focus on event management, MAD excels in coordinating and executing a wide range of activities. The club takes charge of event logistics, sponsorship acquisition, and planning, ensuring a smooth and engaging experience for all participants. MAD's expertise extends to organizing star nights, where renowned artists and performers showcase their talents, captivating the college community. Moreover, the club takes pride in overseeing the cultural festival of the college, curating an immersive experience with diverse performances, competitions, and interactive activities. MAD is the driving force behind creating unforgettable and well-managed events that foster a vibrant and inclusive campus culture.
