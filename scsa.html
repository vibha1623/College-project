SCSA(students club for social activity)

SCSA (Students Club for Social Activity) is a college club committed to making a positive impact in the community through various social initiatives. One of its prominent projects, Shiksha-Abhiyan, is dedicated to providing free education to economically disadvantaged students. The club organizes tutoring sessions, mentoring programs, and educational workshops, ensuring that every child has equal access to quality education. SCSA's volunteers work tirelessly to empower these students, nurturing their academic growth and unlocking their potential. Through Shiksha-Abhiyan, the club aims to bridge the educational gap and create a brighter future for these underprivileged students, fostering social equality and inclusive development in the community.
